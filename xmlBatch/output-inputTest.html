Cheese Global Sales

General sales increased in a 1.6%, from 221484 USD in 2017 to 225034 USD in 2018. Total sold units increased in a 2.15% going from 23374 units to 23876 in 2018.

Cheese Sales By City
The city with the most sales revenue was Paris with incomes worth 73392 USD. However, the city with the biggest amount of sold units was Paris, reporting 7136 sold cheeses. Lyon was the city with the least amount of sales revenue with 21986 USD.

Sales Performance by Product
The Cheese type with the most sales value was Fresh Cheese, with Costeño as the most influential product with a total sale value of 18040 USD. Fresh Cheese was the type with the most sold units, with Costeño as the most sold cheese with 2309 units.
